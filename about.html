About Us

We are a modern Event Planning & Management Platform. Our goal is to help planning agencies and individuals organize events easily and efficiently.

We simplify communication between people, teams, and organizers. Everything is connected in one system so users can collaborate without difficulty.

If a user does not have a team, they can join existing groups of solo participants. This helps people connect with others and build strong teams.

Once enough members gather, they can participate in organized trips or events based on their age category:

• 18 - 30 → Young Group (Travel & Adventure)
• 30 - 50 → Professional Group (Events & Networking)
• 50+ → Senior Group (Comfort Travel & Social Events)

Our platform makes event planning simple, fast, and enjoyable for everyone.
